Vegan brownies

Author: Daria Smolyarova 

Ingredients: 
2 Bananas
2 spoons of peanut butter 
5 spoons of cocoa powder 

Workflow: 
Step 1. In a large bowl, mash 3 ripe bananas.
Step 2. Add peanut butter and stir the mixture until smooth. 
Step 3. Sift in cocoa powder, give it a mix. 
Step 4. Transfer to a brownie pan folded with parchment paper.
Step 5. Bake flourless brownies in hot oven for 22 to 30 minutes
