Hummus
author: LaGerlach

200g dried chickpeas
4 Tbsp Tahini
2 Lemons
2 garlic cloves
4 Tbsp olive oil
water
Salt
1 tsp ground Cumin
black sesame

1. Soak the chickpeas in water overnight.
2. Rinse and drain the chickpeas.
3. Cover the chickpeas with water (no salt) in a pressure cooker and cook them for 20 minutes.
4. Add the soft chickpeas, lemon zest, lemon juice, Tahini, olive oil, ground cumin, salt and garlic to a blender and mix until smooth.
5. Adjust the consistency by adding water and blending.
6. Adjust to taste with tahini, olive oil, salt and cumin.
7. Serve the hummus in a bowl topped with olive oil and black sesame.
