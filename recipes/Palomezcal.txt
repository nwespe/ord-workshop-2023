hector hernandez gonzalez

Ingredients
lime juice (optional)
50ml espadin mezcal
30ml fresh grapefruit juice
10ml agave syrup
ice
soda water
For the garnish
slice of grapefruit

Method
STEP 1
If you're choosing to salt the rim of your glass like a classic margarita, dip the rim in a saucer of lime juice, followed by a saucer of sea salt.

STEP 2
Put all the ingredients except the soda water into a cocktail shaker, fill with ice and give it a good shake.

STEP 3
Strain into a tall collins glass over fresh ice and top with soda water and a slice of grapefruit to garnish.
