Spargel (white asparagus) Ohitashi
- Bring water to boil
- Add 1 tsp sugar, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp butter
- Cook Spargel (white asparagus) until soft
- Cut white asparagus to 3 - 4 cm length
- Soak in Mentsuyu (Japanese soup base)
- Add fish flakes on top
- Cool in fridge for at least 3 hours
- Enjoy!
